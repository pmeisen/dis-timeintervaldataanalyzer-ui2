Get Started: Download, Unzip, and Go

It is easy as this. Download the TidaIs package, unzip it, and get started.
Watch the tutorial videos and get more and more insights, step by step.

Fast Integration

Insert millions of time interval records. Use a data integration tool, the provided front-end, or pump the records directly into the system through the available interfaces.
With a speed being in average between 10,000 and 20,000 records per seconds there is no limit.

Intuitive Query Language

Use the easy to learn TidaQl to formalize your questions to be answered by your data.
Illustrate the results with the available TidaUi, use your favorite tool (e.g., Tableau©) or develop your own visualization.
Thanks to the provided JDBC driver you can query the system from almost any application.

Support of Complex Data Structures

Supporting any kind of dimensions and data structures. No extra effort to overcome any summarizability problems. Insert your business data as it is given to you, by specifying the descriptive information and start analysing it.
